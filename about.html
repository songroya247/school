About UE School

We are on a mission to give every Nigerian student an unfair advantage in their exams — without breaking the bank.

Our Story

UE School was founded after watching too many brilliant students miss university cut-offs — not because they weren't smart, but because quality exam preparation was expensive and inaccessible. We set out to change that. Starting with a small library of JAMB past questions and a simple score tracker, we have grown into a full adaptive learning platform built specifically for the Nigerian exam ecosystem.

Every feature on this platform — the CBT engine, the SmartPath™ suggestions, the topic-by-topic scoring — was designed by people who have sat the same exams, or who have coached students through them.

What We Believe

  • Access before profit. Our lowest plan costs less than a bottle of water a day. We will never put essential learning behind a paywall we can't justify.
  • Data over guesswork. Your practice sessions generate real insights. Our AI tracks every gap so your study time is spent where it matters most.
  • Nigerian students deserve world-class tools. We build for the reality of our users — patchy internet, shared devices, and exams in June.
  • Transparency always. We are honest about what our predictions mean and what they don't.

What We Cover

UE School is built around the three major Nigerian secondary exit and tertiary entry exams:

  • JAMB UTME — full syllabus coverage, 10,000+ past-question bank, score prediction engine.
  • WAEC SSCE — topic-by-topic drills, essay structure guides, theory marking hints.
  • NECO SSCE — parallel content to WAEC with NECO-specific question patterns.
  • Post-UTME — university-specific question sets for popular institutions.
