1. Who We Are

UE School ("we", "us", "our") is an online exam preparation platform serving Nigerian secondary and post-secondary students. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when you use our website and platform at ueschool.ng.

2. Data We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account data — your full name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash via Supabase Auth).
  • Academic profile — the exams you are sitting, your target score, preferred subjects, and exam date. You provide this during sign-up and may update it at any time.
  • Payment data — when you subscribe, payment is processed by Paystack. We receive only a transaction reference and your plan status. We never store card details.
  • Support communications — messages you send us via email or the contact form.

Data we collect automatically

  • Usage data — which topics you practise, CBT session scores, time spent, streak data, and XP accumulated. This data is central to how our adaptive engine works.
  • Device & browser data — browser type, operating system, screen size, and approximate location (country/region level only) collected via server logs.
  • Cookies & local storage — for authentication sessions and user preferences. See the Cookies section below.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account and verify your identity.
  • To personalise your study experience — tracking gaps, surfacing weak topics, and adjusting difficulty via SmartPath™.
  • To process subscription payments and send receipts.
  • To send you important service emails (password resets, billing confirmations). We do not send unsolicited marketing emails without your consent.
  • To improve the platform — aggregated, anonymised usage data helps us identify the questions most students struggle with.
  • To comply with applicable Nigerian data protection regulations (NDPR).

4. Data Sharing

We share personal data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Data is stored on servers located within GDPR-compliant regions.
  • Paystack — payment processing. Governed by Paystack's own Privacy Policy.
  • Legal obligation — if required by Nigerian law or a valid court order.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Passwords are hashed and salted by Supabase Auth — we cannot read your password.
  • Access to the production database is restricted to authorised staff only.

No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Bureau (NDPB).

To exercise any of these rights, visit our Contact page with "Data Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies and browser local storage to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. You can clear cookies via your browser settings at any time; doing so will log you out.

8. Children & Minors

Our platform is primarily used by students aged 14–20. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes their child under 13 has registered, please contact us and we will promptly delete the account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated via email to registered users.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please visit our Contact page.
