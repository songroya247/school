📌 Key Concepts

This lesson covers the foundational algebraic concepts tested in JAMB and WAEC. Pay attention to the factorization method and the quadratic formula — these appear in almost every past paper.

  • 1 Factorization method: express as (ax + b)(cx + d) = 0
  • 2 Quadratic formula: x = (−b ± √(b²−4ac)) / 2a
  • 3 Sum of roots = −b/a · Product of roots = c/a
  • 4 Number base conversions: always divide by target base repeatedly
Key Formula
x = (−b ± √(b²−4ac)) / 2a  |  where a, b, c are coefficients
